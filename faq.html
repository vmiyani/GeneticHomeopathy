Homeopathy is a natural system of medicine developed over 200 years ago by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann. It is based on the principle of "like cures like" — a substance that causes symptoms in a healthy person can, in very small doses, treat similar symptoms in a sick person.


Unlike conventional medicine which often suppresses symptoms, homeopathy stimulates the body's own healing response. Remedies are derived from natural sources — plants, minerals, and animal substances — and are prepared through a careful process of serial dilution and succussion (vigorous shaking), making them extremely gentle yet effective.

Homeopathy works by stimulating the body's vital force — its innate ability to heal itself. When you take a homeopathic remedy that matches your complete symptom picture, it acts as a catalyst to activate your body's natural healing mechanisms.


The remedy is selected based on the totality of your symptoms — physical, mental, and emotional — making each treatment truly individualized. This holistic approach treats the whole person rather than just isolated symptoms.

While both use natural substances, they are fundamentally different systems. Herbal medicine uses material doses of plants for their biochemical effects — similar to how conventional drugs work. Homeopathy uses highly diluted substances based on the principle of "like cures like."


Homeopathic remedies are prepared through a specific process of serial dilution and succussion which makes them extremely safe with no toxic side effects, while herbal medicines can have interactions and side effects similar to conventional drugs.

Homeopathic remedies are prepared in various potencies (dilution levels), commonly expressed as C (centesimal) or X (decimal) scales. For example, 6C means the original substance has been diluted 1:100 six times and succussed at each step.


Common potencies include: 6C/12C (low potency, good for acute conditions and self-care), 30C (medium potency, most commonly used), and 200C and above (high potency, typically prescribed by practitioners). Your homeopath will recommend the right potency for your specific needs.
